Highlights

  • Tight shooter mechanics
  • Introduced aim assist mechanic for ease of controls
  • Amazing pvp splitscreen multiplayer

Lowlights

  • Rushed level design making missions feel repetitive
  • No online multiplayer on original console
  • The look and feel of the Alien franchise are captured perfectly

Why we love this game

Halo had perfectly balanced combat with satisfying shooting mechanics

You really felt like a super soldier trying to keep your team of marines alive.

Not only did this game have an engaging campaign, but it also had plenty of multiplayer maps and game modes that combined awesome vehicle physics with tight shooting controls

Overall: Its one of the most influential shooter games because it introduced aim assist mechanics to console making shooter games accessible to controller players while also pushing graphics and physics
